Cookies Policy

Last Updated: [Date]

KYLE PHARM K. LTD ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies on kyle.co.ke. By using our Service, you consent to the use of cookies.

Our Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use cookies, how third-parties we may partner with may use cookies on the Service, your choices regarding cookies, and further information about cookies.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of text sent by your web browser by a website you visit. A cookie file is stored in your web browser and allows the Service or a third-party to recognize you and make your next visit easier and the Service more useful to you.

2. How KYLE PHARM K. LTD uses cookies

Since our website is hosted on GitHub Pages and uses third-party tools for prescriptions, we use cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies:

These are necessary for the website to function properly. They include cookies used by our hosting provider (GitHub) to ensure the site loads quickly and securely via HTTPS.

Functionality Cookies:

These allow us to remember choices you make when you use our website, such as remembering your details on a form so you don't have to re-enter them if the page reloads.

Analytics Cookies:

We may use tools like Google Analytics to collect information about how you use our website (e.g., which pages you visit most). This data is anonymous and helps us improve the pharmacy experience.

3. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-parties cookies to report usage statistics of the Service and deliver advertisements on and through the Service.

JotForm / Cognito Forms:

When you use our "Upload Prescription" feature, the form provider may place cookies to ensure the security of your upload and prevent spam.

WhatsApp:

If you use the "Chat on WhatsApp" button, WhatsApp may store cookies on your device to facilitate the chat session.

Google Maps:

We use Google Maps to show our location. Google uses cookies to remember your preferences and for security.

4. Your choices regarding cookies

If you would like to delete cookies or instruct your web browser to delete or refuse cookies, please visit the help pages of your web browser.

Please note, however, that if you delete cookies or refuse to accept them, you might not be able to use all of the features we offer (such as the prescription upload form), and some of our pages might not display properly.

For Chrome: Visit the Google Chrome Help page.
For Safari: Visit the Apple Support page.
For Firefox: Visit the Mozilla Firefox Help page.

5. More Information

For more information about the Data Protection Act of Kenya and your digital rights, you can visit the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) website.
